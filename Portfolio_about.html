Hey! I'm Ben, welcome to this little page I made. I was born to Isreali / Brazilian parents and we moved to NYC when I was 2. In 2013 I moved to Melbourne to attend La Trobe Univercity. After graduating with a BA in International Development as well as doing a Permaculture Design Course (PDC), I went to and island off the southern coast of Cambodia to work for an NGO there called Friends of Koh Rong and worked with a local community teaching english and sustainable designs. I listen to a lot of music, this page design was actually inspired by the album Traum Collection (2014) by Max Cooper, a musician that has never ceased to blow my mind. I love discovering new artists, attempting to design things, been to a few different countries and have always been keen to learn and push myself.
