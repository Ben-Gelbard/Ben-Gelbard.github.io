Hey! I'm Ben, welcome to this little page I made. I was born to Israeli / Brazilian parents and we moved to NYC when I was 2. I spent most of my development years in NYC (except for half of my highschool years which were spent in Brazil). After high school I decided to be somehwat of a nomad, I spent a few years working odd jobs from making promotional videos for small NGOs to working in small shops, with a good habit of getting stuck in places for extended periods of time. In 2013 I moved to Melbourne to attend La Trobe Univercity. After graduating with a BA in International Development as well as doing a Permaculture Design Course (PDC), I went to and island off the southern coast of Cambodia to work for an NGO there called Friends of Koh Rong and worked with a local community teaching english and sustainable designs. I've returned to Australia since then have been studying coding and programming.
